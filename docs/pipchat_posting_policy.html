Posting Policy & User Liability

Rules governing what users may post on PipChat
For the people, By the people.
PipChat – Publicly Interactive Post Chat

USER RESPONSIBILITY & LIABILITY

You are solely responsible for the content you post on PipChat. The platform does not pre-screen or endorse user-generated material. By posting, you explicitly agree that **any legal consequences, civil liability, or disputes arising from your content are entirely your responsibility**.

You agree to indemnify and hold harmless PipChat, ScatterBrain, its employees, and contractors from any claims or damages related to your content.

1. Platform Intent & Guiding Principles

PipChat is designed for open, constructive, community-driven public conversation. To maintain a safe environment, all posted content must follow these rules.

2. Prohibited & Illegal Content

Posts may NOT contain:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content involving minors sexually.
  • Promotion, coordination, or glorification of terrorism or violent extremism.
  • Illegal drug sale or distribution.
  • Sexually explicit content (especially involving minors).
  • Incitement to violence, threats, or calls for physical harm.
  • Content violating copyright/trademark laws.
  • Financial scams, harmful challenges, criminal instructions, or hacking guidance.

3. Accuracy, Misinformation & Public Harm

You agree to avoid posting:

  • Rumors, fabricated claims, or unverified accusations.
  • Misinformation intended to mislead or incite panic.
  • False claims that may damage individuals, communities, or public order.

4. Harassment, Abuse & Hate Speech

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, bullying, stalking, or targeted hateful behavior.
  • Attacks on protected characteristics (race, religion, orientation, disability, etc.).
  • Encouraging others to harass or harm someone.

5. Privacy, Safety & Doxing Rules

You may NOT post:

  • Any personal information (PII) of individuals or entities without their explicit consent.
  • Home addresses, phone numbers, private emails, financial/medical data, etc.
  • Doxing attempts, threats to expose someone’s identity, or blackmail.

6. Enforcement & Moderation

PipChat may take any of these actions:

  • Immediate removal of violating content.
  • Temporary or permanent limitations on posting.
  • Account suspension or termination.
  • Reporting to legal authorities where required.

Moderators may edit, hide, or delete posts at any time to enforce policy compliance.

7. Minors & Underage Posting Restrictions

These rules apply:

  • Under 13: Cannot use PipChat.
  • 13–15 (Under 16): Must have a parent-linked account. Cannot create replies or comments. All posts must be approved by a parent/guardian.
  • 16–17: May face reduced permissions; may require parent approval for posting.

Parents/guardians are fully accountable for the activity of their linked minor accounts.

8. Consent & Acceptance

By posting on PipChat, you acknowledge and fully accept this Posting Policy and consent to the platform’s moderation, enforcement, and safety processes.

9. Thank You

Thank you for helping to keep PipChat constructive, respectful, and safe. Your cooperation ensures healthy public discourse for everyone.
