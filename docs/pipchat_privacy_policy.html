Core Agreement

1. Terms of Service

These Terms of Service (“Terms”) govern your use of PipChat – Publicly Interactive Post Chat Application (the “App”) and any related websites, services, or tools operated by ScatterBrain (“we”, “us”, “our”). By creating an account or using PipChat, you agree to these Terms.

1.1 Eligibility & Account Creation

To use PipChat you must be legally allowed to enter into a binding agreement in your jurisdiction. Minors may only use PipChat under the conditions described in the Age-Gated Access Policy.

  • You must provide accurate information during registration (including email, phone, birth year).
  • You must keep your login credentials secure and not share them with others.
  • You are responsible for all activity under your account.

1.2 Parent-Linked Minor Accounts

If your birth year indicates that you are a minor (under 18), you may only use PipChat with a linked parent or guardian account who has explicitly approved your account and your posts. Additional age restrictions apply:

  • Under 16: You cannot create replies/comments. You may only post if a linked parent/guardian approves your account and each post.
  • 16–17: You may have a PipChat account linked to a parent/guardian. Post creation may require parent approval based on app settings and applicable law.
  • Parent/guardian accounts must be created by adults who take legal responsibility for the minor’s use.

We may take steps (including account suspension) if we discover an account misrepresenting age or failing to comply with these age rules.

1.3 Service Description & Ephemeral Content

PipChat is a public, time-bound social platform for sharing posts, threads, community discussions, and chats. Certain content types (posts, communities, chat threads) automatically expire based on:

  • The user’s subscription plan or account type.
  • Platform-wide lifetime rules (e.g., within a few days).
  • Moderation actions and safety flags which may accelerate expiry.

Expired content will no longer be visible in the app to general users but may be retained internally for safety, audit, and legal reasons as described in the Data Retention section.

1.4 Acceptable Use

You must comply with the Posting Policy and all applicable laws. You agree not to:

  • Engage in illegal, harmful, or fraudulent activity.
  • Harass, bully, threaten, or incite violence against others.
  • Post content that violates intellectual property or privacy rights.
  • Attempt to bypass security, abuse flags, or game the scoring system.

1.5 Plans, Billing & Payments

PipChat may offer free and paid plans with different features (e.g., longer post/community lifetimes, additional tools, verification). Payments may be processed via:

  • Google Play Billing
  • Apple In-App Purchases (IAP)
  • Stripe
  • Razorpay

We do not store full payment card numbers. Billing terms (pricing, renewal, refunds) may be governed by the relevant platform’s terms (e.g., Google Play, App Store) and our own plan-specific terms.

1.6 Suspension & Termination

We may suspend or terminate accounts, communities, or content if:

  • You violate these Terms or the Posting Policy.
  • You engage in abusive, fraudulent, or deceptive behavior.
  • We are required by law or receive legally valid requests from authorities.

We may, but are not obligated to, notify you of reasons for suspension/termination where legally allowed.

1.7 Disclaimers & Limitation of Liability

PipChat is provided on an “AS IS” and “AS AVAILABLE” basis. To the maximum extent permitted by law:

  • We do not warrant uninterrupted or error-free service.
  • We are not liable for user-generated content or actions of other users.
  • Our aggregate liability to you for any claims related to the Service will be limited to the greater of: (i) the amount you paid us in the last 12 months, or (ii) a reasonable fixed cap as defined by applicable law.

Data Protection

2. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PipChat and ScatterBrain (“we”, “us”) collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our App and related services. It is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR (where applicable) and CCPA/CPRA (for California residents).

2.1 What We Collect

2.1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Birth year
  • Username (internal to PipChat)
  • Profile picture
  • Government ID (where required for verification)
  • Business/government verification documents (for verified accounts)

2.1.2 Location Information

  • GPS-based location when you are creating a post and do not manually select a location.
  • Manually selected map location when you choose a custom location for a post.

PipChat does not continuously track your location in the background. Location is used contextually for posting and ranking relevant content.

2.1.3 User-Generated Content

We process the content you create, including:

  • Public posts and threads
  • Community content
  • Chat messages and replies
  • Comments
  • Images or media files (viewable only when users tap “show more”)

2.1.4 Safety & Scoring Data

To maintain trust and safety, we may derive or store:

  • Verification score and status
  • Community trust score and moderation score
  • Flag counts (content reported by or against you)
  • Abuse patterns, spam indicators, device risk signals

2.1.5 Device & Technical Data

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address and general network information
  • Crash logs and performance analytics

2.1.6 Payments & Subscriptions

When you purchase a plan, we receive:

  • Transaction ID and status
  • Plan or subscription tier
  • Billing platform (Google Play, App Store, Stripe, Razorpay)

We do not store full payment card numbers.

2.2 How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain PipChat and its core features.
  • Display public posts, communities, chats, and replies.
  • Determine local relevance for posts using location.
  • Verify user authenticity, including government/business verification.
  • Maintain user scores, trust levels, and moderation history.
  • Process payments, manage plans, and prevent fraud.
  • Respond to support requests and issues.
  • Improve and evolve PipChat using analytics and feedback.

2.3 Legal Bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent – e.g., when you provide optional information or agree to location use.
  • Contract – to deliver the services you request via the App.
  • Legitimate Interest – security, fraud prevention, abuse detection, analytics.
  • Legal Obligation – complying with laws, responding to valid legal requests.

2.4 Sharing of Information

We may share data in these cases:

  • Service providers: cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, payments, content scanning, customer support tools.
  • Legal and safety: with law enforcement or authorities when we must comply with valid legal requests or prevent serious harm.
  • Other users: your public content, display name, profile picture, and public scores may be visible to others.

We do not sell your personal information. Where “sale” or “sharing” has a specific legal meaning (e.g., under CCPA/CPRA), we operate in compliance and provide opt-out where required.

2.5 Retention & Expired Content

Posts, communities, and chat content may automatically expire from public view within a few days based on plan-specific rules or moderation decisions. However, we may retain expired content internally:

  • For security, fraud, and abuse detection.
  • To respond to legal requests and investigations.
  • To analyze systemic abuse patterns (e.g., spam networks).

In some cases, we may allow users to retrieve their own expired content (e.g., posts expired less than six months ago with no replies and no flags).

2.6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal/safety exceptions).
  • Request export of your data (data portability).
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at scatterbrain@gmail.com. We may ask you to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

2.7 Children & Minors

PipChat is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If we learn that a user is under 13 without verified parent/guardian oversight, we will delete the account.

For minors aged 13–17, additional rules apply as described in the Age-Gated Access Policy, including compulsory parent account linking and posting restrictions (no replies/comments for users under 16).

2.8 Security

We use reasonable and industry-aligned security measures, including encryption in transit, secure storage, hashed passwords, access controls, and fraud detection systems. However, no system is completely secure.

2.9 International Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in different countries, including outside your country of residence. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to protect your data in cross-border transfers.

Rules for Content

3. Posting Policy (User Content Rules)

To maintain a safe environment, all users must respect this Posting Policy. You must explicitly accept these rules before posting using the PipChat composer.

3.1 General Principles

  • Do not harm others or put them at risk.
  • Do not break the law or encourage others to do so.
  • Respect privacy, dignity, and safety of all people.
  • Understand that you are responsible for what you post and share.

3.2 Strictly Prohibited Content

You may not post, share, or promote content that includes:

  • Child sexual abuse material or any sexual content involving or depicting minors.
  • Content that exploits or endangers minors.
  • Incitement to violence, self-harm, or suicide.
  • Hate speech targeting individuals or groups based on protected characteristics.
  • Harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery or extremely explicit sexual content.
  • Instructions for illegal acts or serious harm (e.g., building weapons, hacking, terrorism).
  • Fraudulent scams, phishing, or impersonation of other individuals or entities.
  • Spam, misleading or manipulated media, or deceptive promotions.
  • Malware, hacking tools, or unauthorized access instructions.

3.3 Sensitive & Regulated Content

  • Do not share personal identifiable information of others without explicit consent.
  • Do not dox or encourage harassment against any individual or entity.
  • Do not share medical, financial, or highly sensitive personal data of others in a way that could harm them.
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights.

3.4 Media & “Show More” Safety Layer

PipChat uses a deliberate “no direct media auto-display” model. Media such as images or videos are hidden behind a “show more” control. This:

  • Reduces accidental exposure to sensitive or undesired content.
  • Adds a friction layer before users view potentially graphic media.

3.5 Public Trust & Score System

To help users assess credibility, PipChat displays public trust and moderation scores based on factors like:

  • Verification status (email, phone, government ID, business/gov docs).
  • History of posts and comments.
  • Flags received and moderation actions.
  • Community feedback and behavior patterns.

Abusive or low-quality behavior will reduce public trust scores. Severe or repeated violations can lead to suspension, bans, or legal escalation.

3.6 Responsibility & Liability

You acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are solely responsible for the content you publish.
  • Other users and third parties may rely on your posts at their own risk.
  • ScatterBrain may remove or restrict content and accounts that violate this policy or applicable law without further notice.
Enforcement

4. Content Moderation Policy

Our moderation system combines automated detection, community flagging, and human review (where necessary) to keep PipChat safe and usable.

4.1 Flagging & Reporting

Users can flag:

  • Posts and threads
  • Communities
  • Chat messages and replies
  • Users (e.g., for abuse or spam)

The system may enforce rules such as:

  • Limiting how often one user can flag the same content or community.
  • Reducing remaining lifetime of communities based on flag counts.
  • Hiding content or restricting visibility pending review.

4.2 Automated & Third-Party Tools

We may use automated systems and third-party processors to detect:

  • Spam and bots
  • Harmful or explicit content
  • Malicious behaviors and patterns

These tools assist human moderators but do not replace our judgment. We may refine them as PipChat grows.

4.3 Moderation Outcomes

Enforcement actions may include (non-exhaustive):

  • Soft warnings or educational notices.
  • Content removal or hiding.
  • Limiting posting or reply abilities (rate limiting or temporary muting).
  • Reducing user trust score or marking accounts as high-risk.
  • Suspending or banning accounts and communities.
  • Cooperating with law enforcement where there is risk of serious harm.

4.4 Appeals

Where feasible, we may provide a way for you to appeal moderation decisions by contacting support at scatterbrain@gmail.com. We cannot guarantee reversal and are not obligated to restore content removed for safety or legal reasons.

Minor Safety

7. Age-Gated Access & Minor Safety Policy

PipChat is designed with strict protections for minors. We use birth year and account relationships (parent/guardian links) to enforce age-based restrictions.

7.1 Under 13

  • PipChat is not intended for children under 13.
  • If we become aware of an under-13 account, we will delete or fully disable it.
  • We will remove associated data as required by law, subject to safety/legal holds.

7.2 Ages 13–15 (Under 16)

  • A valid parent or guardian account must be linked to the minor’s account.
  • The parent/guardian must approve the minor’s account.
  • The parent/guardian must approve post creation; minors cannot post independently where this is enforced.
  • Users under 16 are not allowed to write replies/comments, even if they can create posts with parent approval.
  • PipChat may show indicators to the parent/guardian for oversight (e.g., activity summaries).

7.3 Ages 16–17

  • A parent/guardian account link may still be required, depending on local laws and app settings.
  • Post creation and some actions may require parental approval or may be more lightly supervised compared to younger minors.
  • We may still restrict certain sensitive features to users 18+ only.

7.4 Parent / Guardian Responsibilities

Parents or guardians who link their account to a minor:

  • Agree to monitor and oversee the minor’s use of PipChat.
  • Accept responsibility for the minor’s actions and adherence to the Posting Policy and Terms.
  • Can revoke consent or request account deletion at any time (subject to verification).

7.5 Verification & False Information

We may take action if we suspect that:

  • A user has misrepresented their birth year to bypass age rules.
  • A parent/guardian account is not legitimate or is being misused.

Actions may include limiting features, requiring additional verification, or suspending the account(s).
